MoLog
Stable release
Transporter 3 / March 10, 2009 [1]
Operating system BD-J
Platform Blu-ray Disc
Type Movie Blogging
Website MoLog's Official Online Home Page

MoLog, or Movie Log is a BD-Live feature. This interactive feature allows the viewer to participate in online discussions regarding Blu-ray titles. This online discussion community can be accessible from a BD-Live player and blogging can be done while the film is being viewed or as a standalone feature. Viewers can also access MoLog from any home computer with an internet connection.[2]
